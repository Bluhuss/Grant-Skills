    OBJECTIVES

    • To enable every parent, guidance and individuals achieve their goal towards their desired educational taste for themselves, children and relatives.
    • To make a massive drive towards eradication of illiteracy and out of school children/individual as education should be everyone's right.
    • To curb the rate at which students drop out of school due to not being able to purchase some mandatory handouts and other school requirements which finance can solve.
    • To make sure every child has access to classroom with adequate study materials and food for sustainability; as this will take away children from hawking in the streets.
    • To give the less priviledged access to quality education of any level through our foundation.
